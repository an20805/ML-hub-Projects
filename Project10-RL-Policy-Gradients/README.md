# Project 10: Deep Reinforcement Learning with Policy Gradients

## Objective
Optimize and train neural policies directly for complex environments.

## Dataset
**OpenAI Gym Continuous & Control Environments:** Simulation environments for training neural policies using policy optimization methods.

## Algorithms & Techniques
- Policy Networks
- Reward-to-Go
- REINFORCE
- Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO)

## What You'll Implement
Implement REINFORCE and PPO algorithms to train neural policies for sequential decision-making tasks.

## Key Learnings & Takeaways
Learn how modern policy gradient methods optimize neural network policies to master complex environments.
